What this update changes:
- Keeps Version 37.1 multiple-file uploading.
- Uses website analysis and available Instagram analysis to guide the business tone.
- Creates three deliberately different caption options:
  Engaging
  Informative
  Enquiry focused
- Informative captions can include useful verified business details.
- Enquiry focused captions can end with the website when relevant.
- The website is only added for Business accounts with a supplied website.
- The website is not forced into every caption.
- Individual accounts never receive an automatic website line.
- Captions avoid dashes and common artificial-intelligence marketing phrases.
- Captions use British English and more natural sentence structures.
- Existing image accuracy rules remain in place.
