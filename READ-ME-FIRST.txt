STRIPE SETUP

1. Create a Business monthly price for £49.
2. Create a Pro monthly price for £99.
3. Copy their price IDs into the Vercel variables above.
4. Create a Stripe webhook endpoint:
   https://picplanrapp.com/api/billing/webhook
5. Subscribe it to:
   checkout.session.completed
   customer.subscription.created
   customer.subscription.updated
   customer.subscription.deleted
6. Copy its signing secret into STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET.
7. Enable the Stripe customer portal.
