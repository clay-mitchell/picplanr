This build keeps the real Instagram publishing introduced in Version 42 and

HOW TO OPEN REVIEW MODE

Use:

https://www.picplanrapp.com/?review=instagram

After login, PicPlanr opens Connected accounts and displays a discreet four-step
review progress strip:

1. Connect Instagram
2. Open Smart calendar
3. Review and approve
4. Publish and open the live post

RECORDING FLOW

• Begin on Connected accounts
• Connect the Instagram professional test account
• Click Continue to Smart calendar
• Open one scheduled post
• Review the selected image and caption
• Tick the explicit approval box
• Click Publish to Instagram now
• Show the large Published successfully result
• Click Open the Instagram post

This mode does not fake or simulate a publish. The final success state only
appears after the real Instagram publishing endpoint returns successfully.

NORMAL CUSTOMERS

The guided strip is only visible when the URL contains:

?review=instagram

The normal PicPlanr interface remains unchanged for ordinary customers.
