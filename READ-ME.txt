PICPLANR VERSION 42.3 — INSTAGRAM MEDIA READY FIX

Replace only:

api/instagram/publish.js

WHY IT FAILED

Meta accepted the image and returned a container ID, but PicPlanr attempted to
publish before Instagram had finished processing that container.

FIX

• Waits for container status FINISHED
• Adds a short safety delay
• Retries Meta error 9007 and subcode 2207027
• Returns a clear timeout or processing error if Meta never becomes ready

No SQL or environment changes are required.
