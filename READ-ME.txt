PICPLANR VERSION 40.6 — NO DASHES IN CAPTIONS

Replace these two files:

index.html
api/picplanr.js

This update applies the rule twice:

1. The artificial intelligence prompt is told never to use any dash or hyphen
   character, including hyphenated compound words.
2. The browser cleans any dash that still slips through before showing the
   caption to the customer.

Examples:

city-centre becomes city centre
behind-the-scenes becomes behind the scenes
venue — available now becomes venue, available now

This applies to feed captions, caption regenerations and Story text.

No Supabase, Stripe or Vercel environment changes are required.
