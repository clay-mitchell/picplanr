PICPLANR VERSION 41.3 — ACCOUNT DELETION

Replace:

index.html
api/account/delete.js

WHAT WAS ADDED

• A small, subtle Account controls section at the bottom of Plan and usage.
• A low emphasis Delete account link.
• A confirmation modal requiring the customer to type DELETE.
• Permanent removal of the authenticated user's PicPlanr account.
• Workspace deletion for a sole workspace owner.
• Active Stripe subscription cancellation before deletion.
• Removal of saved content, account voice, analysis, schedules and usage data
  through the workspace database relationships.
• Protection against deleting a workspace that still has other team members.

No SQL, Stripe or Vercel environment changes are required.

IMPORTANT

Test this with a separate test customer account. Do not test deletion using the
main owner account unless you genuinely intend to permanently remove it.
