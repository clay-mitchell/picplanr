• Instagram connection lookup
• Instagram token decryption
• Temporary image upload
• Publishing record creation
• Instagram media container creation
• Instagram media publish
• Usage recording

After deployment:

1. Try publishing the same test post once.
2. Send a screenshot of the new error message.
3. Do not reconnect Instagram or change Meta settings until the exact stage is
   known.
